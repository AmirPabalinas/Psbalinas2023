Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients: 1/2 cup unsalted butter, 1 cup semisweet or bittersweet chocolate, 1 cup powdered sugar, 2 eggs, 2 egg yolks, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1/4 cup all-purpose flour, pinch of salt.

Directions: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease and flour individual ramekins. Melt butter and chocolate together. Stir in powdered sugar. Add eggs and egg yolks, then vanilla. Fold in flour and salt. Pour batter into ramekins. Bake for 12-14 minutes. Serve warm with a dusting of powdered sugar.

Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients: Chicken breasts, fettuccine, heavy cream, butter, Parmesan cheese.

Directions: Cook chicken until fully done. Boil fettuccine. In a saucepan, melt butter and add heavy cream. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens. Combine cooked chicken and pasta, then pour the sauce over it.

Guacamole with Tortilla Chips

Ingredients: Ripe avocados, tomatoes, red onion, lime juice, cilantro, salt, pepper, tortilla chips.

Directions: Mash avocados. Dice tomatoes and red onion, then add to the mashed avocados. Stir in lime juice, chopped cilantro, salt, and pepper to taste. Serve with tortilla chips.

Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients: Fresh strawberries, shortcakes, whipped cream.

Directions: Wash and slice strawberries. Split shortcakes, layer with strawberries, and top with whipped cream. Serve immediately.

Vegetarian Lasagna

Ingredients: Lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, spinach, mushrooms.

Directions: Cook lasagna noodles. In a baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta cheese, marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, spinach, and mushrooms. Repeat layers. Bake until bubbly and golden.

Fruit Salsa with Cinnamon Chips

Ingredients: Assorted fresh fruits, cinnamon sugar, flour tortillas.

Directions: Dice fruits and mix. Brush tortillas with melted butter, sprinkle with cinnamon sugar, and bake until crispy. Serve with fruit salsa.
