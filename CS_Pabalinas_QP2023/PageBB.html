Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients: Pasta, eggs, cheese, pancetta, black pepper.

Directions: Cook pasta. Meanwhile, fry pancetta until crispy. In a bowl, mix eggs, cheese, and pepper. Toss hot pasta with pancetta, then mix with egg mixture. Serve immediately.

Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients: Chicken breasts, fettuccine, heavy cream, butter, Parmesan cheese.

Directions: Cook chicken until fully done. Boil fettuccine. In a saucepan, melt butter and add heavy cream. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until sauce thickens. Combine cooked chicken and pasta, then pour the sauce over it.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients: Romaine lettuce, croutons, Parmesan cheese, Caesar dressing.

Directions: Toss torn lettuce with croutons and grated Parmesan cheese. Drizzle with Caesar dressing. Mix well and serve.

Grilled Salmon

Ingredients: Salmon fillets, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, herbs.

Directions: Marinate salmon in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, and herbs. Grill until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, tofu or chicken, soy sauce, stir-fry sauce.

Directions: Stir-fry tofu or chicken and mixed vegetables in a hot pan with soy sauce and stir-fry sauce until cooked. Serve over rice.

Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients: Ground beef, hamburger buns, cheese slices, lettuce, tomatoes, onions.

Directions: Form ground beef into patties and grill or cook in a pan until done. Place on a bun, add cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and onions.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients: Assorted fresh fruits, honey, lime juice, mint leaves.

Directions: Cut fruits into bite-sized pieces. Toss with honey and lime juice. Garnish with fresh mint leaves.
